About research.chirag.cloud

research.chirag.cloud is a nonprofit initiative hosted by Chirag Roy. It focuses on connecting and empowering science-based groups, communities, and peers by providing the best technical solutions and support. Our goal is to help these communities grow, collaborate, and reach more people, making science more accessible and impactful for everyone.
